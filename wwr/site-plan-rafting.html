Overview

Purpose

Our site plan outlines the layout and content strategy for the various pages of our website, ensuring a user-friendly experience that effectively communicates our company's identity, offerings, and contact information. Each page is designed with specific objectives in mind, from captivating visitors on the home page to providing comprehensive information about our company, team, and rafting trips. By strategically organizing content and incorporating appealing graphics, calls to action, and user input forms, we aim to engage visitors and encourage them to explore our adventure offerings and get in touch with us.

Audience

‚úÖ Who are the target customers?
Our target customers span a wide range of demographics, including individuals and groups of various ages and backgrounds who are passionate about outdoor activities and seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures. They may include young adults in their 20s and 30s, families looking for bonding experiences, corporate groups seeking team-building activities, and retirees seeking new adventures in their leisure time.

‚úÖ What do they want?
Our target customers seek exhilarating experiences in nature, the opportunity to challenge themselves physically and mentally, and the chance to create lasting memories with friends, family, or colleagues. They desire adventure, excitement, and a break from the routine of daily life.

‚úÖ What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website?
When accessing our website, customers expect to find comprehensive information about our rafting company, including details about our team, the range of trips we offer, available equipment, safety measures, and booking procedures. They also expect to find captivating visuals and testimonials from satisfied customers to inspire confidence in our services.

‚úÖ How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Our audience, spanning various demographics, will likely access our site via mobile phones, tablets, desktops, and laptops. While younger users may favor mobile browsing for its convenience, families and corporate groups may prefer desktops or laptops for detailed planning. Tablets offer a balance between screen size and portability. It's crucial for us to ensure that our website is responsive for optimal user experience across devices."
